What it does

Everything automatic, nothing manual

Matchday reads the official fixture list and handles the rest on its own. After /setup you never touch a button again.

Daily schedule

Every morning it posts a clean board: last night's results with scores, plus what's coming up today — each match in everyone's local time.

Daily 08:30

Auto Discord events

Creates a Guild Scheduled Event for every upcoming match — rolling, always the next ~48 hours. Members hit “Interested” and get pinged at kickoff.

Rolling +48h

A voice channel per match

About 15 minutes before kickoff a temporary 🔴 Team vs Team voice channel appears, so everyone gathers in one place. It's removed automatically after full time.

Temporary

Instant when you add it

Join mid-tournament during a live match? The moment you run /setup, Matchday spins up that match's voice channel and event right away — no waiting for the next one.

New

Kickoff ping

When a match starts it drops a fun “LIVE now” ping to the self-assign @World Cup role — opt in or out with one click, no spam for anyone else.

Opt-in role

Live standings

All 12 groups with wins/draws/losses, goal difference and points — computed from real results. Switches to the knockout bracket automatically.

/standings

Result cards

At full time it posts a crisp image card — final score, halftime, scorers and venue — straight into your results channel.

At full time

Right time, everywhere

Matches run across three host-country time zones. Matchday shows every kickoff as a Discord timestamp, so it's always correct in each member's own local time.

Timezone-safe

One command, any server

No config files, no dashboards. Invite the bot, run /setup and it builds the category, channels and role itself. Works on every server independently.

/setup
Set up in seconds

Run /setup once. Matchday creates the category, text and voice channels, the role and the events — and starts tracking matches right away. No config files, no dashboard.

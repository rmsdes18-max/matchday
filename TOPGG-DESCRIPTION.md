<h1 align="center">Matchday</h1>
<p align="center"><b>The entire FIFA World Cup 2026, automatic in your Discord.</b></p>
<p align="center">A daily schedule, a Discord event and voice channel for every match, full-time result cards and live standings. Add it, run <code>/setup</code>, and just show up.</p>
